Embassy participates in IETF 2013

The Embassy of Burkina Faso participated in 20th edition of International Engineering & Technology Fair (IETF 2013) which was held at the Pragati Maidan New Delhi from 15-17 February 2013 and inaugurated by President of India Mr. Pranab Mukherjee.

State Council announces the final result of the municipal elections.

On March 1, 2013 the State Council announced the results of the Municipal Elections which were held on February 17, 2013. The Congress for Democracy and Progress (CDP), tertains a clear majority with 12,363 seats out of a total of 18,527 seats.
